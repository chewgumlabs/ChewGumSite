Shane Curry is a professional animator with 10 years experience in primetime television and streaming. Independent cartoons spanning 2D and 3D, with rigging and systems built in Blender, Unreal, ToonBoom, Unity, Godot, Roblox, and most recently vanilla JavaScript and C (with the help of AI). Currently building his own Vector Based Animation Software.

Dead Beat: a playable Rust and WebAssembly room folded directly into the blog as a live experiment.

Falling Hall: the software-rendered impulse pointed downward, an endless faceted shaft with bell chimes on impact.

Phosphor: an oscilloscope-style DOOM raycaster. Every wall is a vector beam. Every corner glows. Old frames decay.

Working notes and longer thought chunks live on the blog. Newest first; experiments highlighted in yellow.
