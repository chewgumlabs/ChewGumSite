Migration is a browser rhythm game by Shelly Xiong. Its public itch.io page credits Shane Curry's music for campaign tracks and ChewGum Time Chime as the audio toolkit.

The public credit makes Migration a verified external use of Shane Curry's music, not just an internal note about where a song might have been used.

The same page credits ChewGum Time Chime as the audio toolkit, making Migration a public bridge between the music lane and the repo-backed tool lane. On this site, that credit resolves to the ChewGumTimeChime public repository note.
