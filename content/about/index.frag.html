Shane Curry is best understood as an animator plus systems builder. The creative and technical sides are not separate tracks here; they are the same practice viewed from different angles.

Shane has 10 years of experience in primetime television and streaming. Independent work spans 2D and 3D animation, rigging, animation systems, and browser-based experiments.

Tool and engine experience on this site includes Blender, Unreal Engine, ToonBoom Harmony, Unity, Godot, Roblox, vanilla JavaScript, and C.

AI is part of the current workflow for research, coding, and prototyping, especially where local tools can accelerate software exploration around animation ideas.

ChewGum Animation is the flagship direction on the site: animation software, animation systems, and related public experiments.

Lab / Toys is where small visual research pages live when they demonstrate a real principle clearly enough to earn a durable URL.

Lab / Tools is where small public software artifacts live when a toy or experiment earns a repo-backed source trail.

The full public credits list on this site includes American Dad!, She-Ra and the Princesses of Power, Trolls: The Beat Goes On!, Duncanville, and HouseBroken.
