The public CHEW GUM campaign trail belongs beside this animation record. Kicktraq mirrors the 2023 Kickstarter campaign as CHEW GUM: An Animated Musical Series by The Paper Machine, with funding unsuccessful, 163 backers, and $12,010 pledged toward a $30,000 goal across May 1-June 30, 2023.

The same Kicktraq update feed records the June 8 campaign update where CHEW GUM was selected as a Kickstarter Project We Love.

A separate CHEW GUM short, "CHEW GUM" | A Short Film, appears in the Animation Block Party 2023 public lineup. Animation Block Party lists Chew Gum by Shane Curry in Shorts Program One at BAM on August 19, 2023.
