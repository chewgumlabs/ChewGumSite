Animation here does not only mean finished films. It also means the rigs, tools, interaction models, and software experiments that make animated work possible.

Shane Curry has worked across primetime television, streaming, and independent animation. The technical side includes rigs and systems built in Blender, Unreal Engine, ToonBoom Harmony, Unity, Godot, Roblox, vanilla JavaScript, and C.

AI is part of the current workflow for local research, coding, and prototyping. It supports the work, but it does not replace animation taste, direction, or judgment.
