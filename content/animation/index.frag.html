Independent animation work, tools, and systems by Shane Curry.

Falling Hall: a software-rendered faceted tunnel that feels like dropping through a bell-lined hole.

From 2016 to the present, Shane has been animating independently in various mediums, building rigs and systems in Blender, Unreal Engine, ToonBoom Harmony, Unity, Godot, and most recently in vanilla JavaScript and C (with the help of AI).
